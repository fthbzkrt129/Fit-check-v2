# Energy And Consistency

This theme tracks the relationship between perceived energy, mental clarity, and the ability to show up consistently for meaningful work.

## Current Synthesis

The first source in this wiki suggests that consistency may depend less on having high energy and more on entering work before attention is contaminated by reactive input. In other words, clean attention may be a more important predictor of consistency than feeling energized ([[2026-04-11-morning-focus-note]], [[deep-work]]).

## Evidence

- The 2026-04-11 journal note explicitly distinguishes cleaner attention from raw energy and reports better work quality in the first focused block of the day ([[2026-04-11-morning-focus-note]]).

## Active Tensions

- It is still unclear whether the key variable is morning timing itself or the absence of interruption and ambiguity.

## Open Questions

- What routines make consistency more repeatable without depending on mood?
- Which behaviors reliably preserve clean attention?
